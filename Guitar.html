Ace Instruments guitars offers an innovative, slim body, round shoulder cutaway guitar with all the traditional build techniques that go into each acoustic guitar. A Solid Sitka Spruce top with traditional hand scalloped bracing is joined by beautiful walnut back and sides which provide a full and balanced tonal range and superior clarity between each string.

The Custom Ace Argent is made of Canadian oak wood, with a bolt on neck to body. A 3/4 scale allows for a chunkier tone while struming. This is a great instrument for beginners to intermediate players, with prices ranging from $350 - $500

The Ace mockingbird is a limited editon guitar, hand carved from mahogany wood. With an inline neck to body and full scale. From deep to high end tones nothing is lost! this is a one of a kind instrument! $1000 - $1200
